About me

My name is Alle Harsha Vardhan, born on May 14, 2012, in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. My father, Srinivas, works as an engineer in a private company, while my mother, Swapna, is a housewife. I have two elder sisters: Keerthi Harsha, who is 7 years older than me, and Nitya Harsha, who is 3 years older. Additionally, I have a cousin brother named Anil Kumar, who resides with us and is an integral part of our family. Anil introduced me to badminton, sparking my journey in the sport.

My journey

In September 5, 2020, I embarked on my badminton journey, inspired by my brother Anil, who had previously participated in the South Zone Nationals, albeit falling short in the quarter-finals. Under his guidance, I delved into the sport, recognizing it as my calling. Initially, I honed my skills at a local academy called iSports, dedicating myself to daily evening practice sessions. Recognizing my potential, Harsha Sports Management, the organization behind iSports, selected me for coaching at their official academy, MK's Academy (short for Malka Komaraiah's Badminton Academy). Under the tutelage of Chief Coach Vikas Harsha, I progressed from a basic to an advanced level.

After a year and a half of rigorous training, I participated in my first tournament in March 2022 in Bangalore, reaching the semi-finals. However, in January 2023, I faced a setback due to overexertion, injuring my right knee. Following a two-month recovery period, I resumed training but struggled to regain my previous form. Consequently, I made the decision to switch to BBBA (Bhasker Babu Badminton Academy) after successful trials in April 10, 2023. Adjusting to life near the academy, I commenced training on May 1st, filled with enthusiasm.

Within months, I competed in my first nationals in Bihar, followed by another tournament in Bhubaneswar, where I faced early exits. Despite setbacks, I remained resilient, aiming for improvement. Subsequent tournaments, including the one in Hyderabad in January, tested my perseverance. Undeterred, I continued to strive for excellence, gearing up for the upcoming nationals in Chhattisgarh.
